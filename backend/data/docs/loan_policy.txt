FINSIGHT LOAN POLICY 2024

1. PERSONAL LOAN ELIGIBILITY
To be eligible for a personal loan, an applicant must:
- Be at least 21 years of age.
- Have a minimum credit score of 600.
- Have a steady income source for at least the last 6 months.
- Maintain a debt-to-income ratio of less than 40%.

2. INTEREST RATES
- Personal Loans: 5.5% - 12% APR based on creditworthiness.
- Home Loans: 3.5% - 5.0% APR (Variable).
- Car Loans: 4.0% - 7.0% APR.

3. PREPAYMENT PENALTY
FinSight does NOT charge any prepayment penalty for personal loans closed before the tenure. However, a flat fee of $50 applies for home loan foreclosures within the first year.

4. LATE PAYMENT FEES
A late fee of $25 is charged if the EMI is not paid within 3 days of the due date. Continued non-payment for 90 days will result in the account being classified as NPA (Non-Performing Asset).

5. DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED
- Identity Proof: Passport, Driver's License, or National ID.
- Address Proof: Utility Bill (not older than 3 months).
- Income Proof: Last 3 months' bank statements or salary slips.
